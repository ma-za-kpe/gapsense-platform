# Pitch Deck Slide Title: Professional Replacement

**Date**: March 19, 2026

---

## ❌ UNPROFESSIONAL TITLE

**"Why GapSense Wins"**

### Problems:
- Too aggressive/competitive for social impact pitch
- "Wins" implies others "lose" (not collaborative)
- Sounds arrogant to UNICEF judges
- Not evidence-based or humble

---

## ✅ RECOMMENDED REPLACEMENT

**"Proven Foundation"**

### Why This Works:
- **Evidence-based**: You HAVE 85% accuracy, production deployment
- **Humble**: "Foundation" = readiness to scale, not claiming victory
- **UNICEF-aligned**: Echoes "foundational learning" mission
- **Credible**: Backed by ViztaEdu (Cohort 3), production links
- **Collaborative**: Positions you as partner, not competitor

---

## ALTERNATIVE PROFESSIONAL TITLES

### Option 1: **"Our Competitive Position"**
**Tone**: Neutral, VC-standard
**Use When**: Judges expect traditional pitch structure
**Content Focus**: Market positioning, barriers to entry

### Option 2: **"Key Differentiators"**
**Tone**: Consulting/strategy language
**Use When**: Want to sound analytical, not boastful
**Content Focus**: Unique capabilities vs. existing solutions

### Option 3: **"Technical Advantages"**
**Tone**: Engineering-first, GitHub credibility
**Use When**: Emphasizing production deployment vs. prototypes
**Content Focus**: AWS live, 4 countries, open-source ready

### Option 4: **"Why This Team"**
**Tone**: People-focused, partnership-driven
**Use When**: Highlighting Maku + ViztaEdu partnership
**Content Focus**: MEST alumni, Cohort 3 connection, Ghana-based

### Option 5: **"Built to Scale"**
**Tone**: Operational readiness
**Use When**: Showing grant-ready infrastructure
**Content Focus**: Production deployed, multi-country, WhatsApp native

### Option 6: **"Evidence of Execution"**
**Tone**: Results-first, no-nonsense
**Use When**: Want to emphasize "done, not just designed"
**Content Focus**: 85% accuracy, $0.05 cost, ~80s latency

## IF JUDGES ASK "WHY NOT SAY 'WINS'?"

**Good Answer**:
"We're not here to 'win' against other applicants. We're here to show we've built a strong foundation that UNICEF can scale. The real win is when Ghana's 84% numeracy gap starts closing. That's the only competition that matters."

**Bad Answer**:
"We're better than everyone else." (Arrogant)
